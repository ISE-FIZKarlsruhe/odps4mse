# Roadmap

This repository is an evolving collection of **Ontology Design Patterns (ODPs)** for Materials Science and Engineering. We actively develop new patterns and welcome community contributions.

---

## Current Patterns

The initial release covers three core modeling requirements:

| Requirement | Focus | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Process Structure** | Processes, sub-processes, steps, execution order | Available |
| **Data & Resources** | Inputs, outputs, parameters (temperature, pressure, instruments) | Available |
| **Project & Roles** | Goals, stages, agents, roles (synthesis, microscopy, simulation) | Available |

These patterns are derived from ontologies including **P-PLAN**, **PMDcore**, **M4I**, **OPMW**, **GPO**, **OntoCAPE**, **STATO**, and **WILD**.

---

## Planned Patterns

We are working on additional ODPs to cover more aspects of MSE modeling:

- **Material Composition Patterns** — Represent chemical compositions, phases, microstructures, and material hierarchies.
- **Measurement & Characterization Patterns** — Model experimental techniques (XRD, SEM, TEM), measurement setups, and result interpretation.
- **Simulation & Computational Patterns** — Capture simulation workflows, computational parameters, software tools, and model validation.
- **Provenance & Reproducibility Patterns** — Track data lineage, experimental reproducibility, and FAIR data principles.
- **Standards & Compliance Patterns** — Align with domain standards (DIN, ISO, ASTM) and regulatory requirements.

## How You Can Help

We encourage the community to contribute new patterns, especially from domains that are currently underrepresented.

1. **Propose a pattern** — Open an [issue](https://github.com/ISE-FIZKarlsruhe/odps4mse/issues) describing the modeling challenge and your proposed solution.
2. **Submit a pattern** — Follow the [contributing guide](contributing.md) and use the [pattern template](https://github.com/ISE-FIZKarlsruhe/odps4mse/blob/main/Patterns/template.md).
3. **Evaluate existing patterns** — Test patterns against your use cases and share feedback.
4. **Add ontologies** — Suggest ontologies from which new patterns should be extracted.

---

## Long-Term Vision

This repository aims to become a **community-maintained catalog** of ODPs for the broader materials science ecosystem, connecting to initiatives like:

- [NFDI4Ing](https://nfdi4ing.de/) — National Research Data Infrastructure for Engineering
- [PMD](https://www.2800-pmd.de/) — Platform Material Digital
- [MatWerk](https://nfdi-matwerk.de/) — NFDI for Materials Science and Engineering
- [EMMC](https://emmc.eu/) — European Materials Modelling Council

By sharing and standardizing design patterns, we reduce redundant modeling effort and improve interoperability across MSE knowledge graphs.
